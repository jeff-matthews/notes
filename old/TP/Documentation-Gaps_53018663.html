Goals

  • Evaluate breadth of content on the AffiniPay partner/developer site against competitor sites (e.g., Coinbase, Stripe, Braintree, Amazon Payments) 
  • Analyze support request emails from the AffiniPay partner/developer community
  • Solicit feedback from AffiniPay staff on common partner/developer support issues
  • Identify and enumerate content gaps; create corresponding issues in TP backlog
  • Create and publish new content

Background and strategic fit

Phases 1 and 2 of the doc site were focused on designing a new partner/developer doc site, building out the site and infrastructure, and migrating existing content to the new site. Now that phase 1 is complete and phase 2 is pending QA, the next step is to start adding new content to the site so that it becomes a high-value resource to current and potential partners/developers.

Assumptions

  • Currently available content lacks adequate coverage
  • Developer sites of competing payment processing technologies can offer insight into what type of content is commonly provided and what we should be providing
  • Identifiying and filling content gaps is the second highest priority for Tech Pubs behind end-of-year doc deliverables for Invoicing
