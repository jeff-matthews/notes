User interaction and design

In our Slack team at work, we get lots of product questions from people working with users. The answers are really useful, but what with reading all the channels, working out what's useful/important, creating a Jira ticket, and writing it up, they just get lost because I don't have time.

So I'm trying out Slack's "reacji channeler" (https://slackhq.com/a-new-way-to-share-messages-across-channels-using-emoji-ba9fdac5744e#.mofrws7po). Ask colleagues to keep an eye out in the Slack channels for information they think should be in the public docs. If they see something, they can tag it with an :addtodocs: emoji (which should be the "Write the Docs" logo, of course!).

Possibly use this to auto-create Jira issues: https://zapier.com/zapbook/zaps/2415/add-new-slack-messages-to-jira-as-new-issues/
